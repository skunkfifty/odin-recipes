Red Sauce

Good for all your red sauce needs

Ingredients

  • 1 LB ground beef
  • 1 Tbs. Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbs Parsley
  • 1 Tbs Basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 large can of crushed tomatoes
  • 12oz tomato paste

Directions

  1. Brown the beef, drain off the fat.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer as long as possible. Stir occasionally.
